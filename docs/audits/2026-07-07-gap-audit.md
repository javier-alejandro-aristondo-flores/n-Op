## A. Verifier-soundness holes (states the oracle could wrongly accept)

**A1 · γ̂ admissibility is never scored. — defect, S2, V1 — disposition: spec-added.**
The PINO emits γ̂ directly, yet no residual checks it is a physical density matrix: hermiticity
`γ̂† = γ̂`, ensemble N-representability `0 ⪯ γ̂ ⪯ 1`, `Tr γ̂ = N_e` (idempotency only for claimed-T=0
states). The research corpus states all of these (`group-B` E.4) but arch-11's Positivity list carried
only `f ∈ [0,1]` / `ρ ≥ 0` — distribution/density language under which the operator bounds are at best
implicit. A candidate that violates them can zero the EOM residuals while being unphysical. Fix landed:
explicit γ̂-admissibility members in Positivity + Conservation (per-k-block spectral bounds are cheap on
the `(Reciprocal, BlockDiag)` encoding; trace is O(bands)).

**A2 · A-field gauge & the electrostatic double-count. — defect, S3 (spec-precision), V1 — disposition: spec-added.**
`E_EM[A] = (1/8π)∫(|E|²+|B|²)` coexists with the Hartree term inside `E_KS`; without a declared gauge
and an explicit longitudinal/transverse partition the electrostatic energy is double-countable and
`EOM/A` is gauge-dependent (research names Runge–Gross gauge fixing; no spec text landed it). Fix landed:
normative arch-05 paragraph — Weyl gauge `A₀ ≡ 0`, transverse (radiation) sector in `E_EM`, longitudinal
/ electrostatic sector owned by Hartree + ion–ion terms.

**A3 · The two "polar" predicates were conflated. — defect, S2 (first fires at Wave 2), V1 — disposition: spec-added (found during the Wave-2 draft).**
`is-polar-material` gated *both* the Fröhlich/POP paths *and* the polarization package (rows
113–119/128). The two properties are independent: Fröhlich needs nonzero Born charges / LO–TO
splitting (a bond property); P_sp/piezo/pyro need a non-centrosymmetric point group. They coincide
on diamond (both false) and wurtzite III-N (both true) — which made the conflation invisible — and
**split on β-Ga₂O₃** (C2/m centrosymmetric ⇒ no P_sp/piezo/pyro, yet its dominant mobility limiter
is a massive multi-mode Fröhlich interaction). Under the old single gate the oracle would either
wrongly activate spontaneous polarization for Ga₂O₃ or wrongly kill its dominant scattering channel.
Fix landed: normative two-predicate paragraph in `arch-13-applicability`
(`is-polar-material` vs `is-noncentrosymmetric`), rows 113–119/128 re-gated
(`arch-09 §9.3`, registry row 128 note).

## B. Missing / incorrect physics for the named scope

**B1 · Pyroelectricity absent. — defect, S2, V1 (III-N flagships) — disposition: spec-added (row) + wave-2 (coefficients).**
The polarization package (rows 113–119) was T-independent. GaN/AlN pyroelectric coefficients
~−(4–7)×10⁻⁶ C/m²K over ΔT ≈ 750 K give ΔP ≈ 3–5×10⁻³ C/m² against n_s·q ≈ 1.6×10⁻² C/m² — a
~20–30% n_s(T) effect vs the ±5% ΔP target; the observable catalog itself lists "polarization drift
with T" as AlN's harsh-env concern. Fix: `pyroelectric-coefficient` registry row (T0/D1,
`is-polar-material`-gated) feeding the 2DEG n_s(T) path; per-material coefficients acquired at Wave 2.

**B2 · Gate-dielectric degradation package empty. — defect, S2, V1 (named scope: Al₂O₃/HfO₂/AlN) — disposition: spec-added (rows) + wave-5 (coefficients).**
B11 covered semiconductor bulk + contacts but nothing for the dielectric layer: no Poole–Frenkel
leakage row (FN row 77 existed; PF — the dominant trap-assisted mechanism at high T — did not), no TDDB
row (E-model / thermochemical Arrhenius, the canonical gate lifetime limiter), no ALD-film
crystallization kinetics (the catalog itself flags "crystallizes above 700 °C → leakage spike" for
ALD Al₂O₃). All closed-form T0/D1 slow-tier/B11 shapes. Fix: rows `poole-frenkel-current`,
`tddb-thermochemical-e-model`, `dielectric-crystallization-jmak`.

**B3 · AlGaN nonlinear polarization interpolation unstated. — defect, S3→S2 at mid-x, V1 — disposition: spec-added (rule) + wave-2 (coefficients).**
CSVs carry bandgap and elastic bowing but no P_sp(x)/e_ij(x) rule; the literature-standard nonlinearity
(Fiorentini–Bernardini nonlinear polarization) shifts ΔP by more than the ±5% target at mid-to-high Al
content. Fix: interpolation rule declared with quadratic-bowing form; coefficients seeded at Wave 2 with
primary sources.

**B4 · Experimental structure channels unmapped. — gap (scope decision taken: in), S3, V1 — disposition: spec-added.**
No XRD structure-factor observable (|F_hkl|² is closed-form from `(h, R_I, Z_I)` + the existing
Debye–Waller machinery) and no Raman observable (Γ-phonon shifts existed; activities need one dχ/du
linear-response row). These are the two most abundant experimental channels an oracle for real crystals
ingests via `Import`. Fix: rows `xrd-structure-factor` and `raman-activity`.

**B5 · Radiation = displacement only. — gap, S3, V1 — disposition: spec-added (exclusion register).**
