# Per-observable accuracy ledger

The per-observable **accuracy regime** вҖ” the target tolerance each closed-form / one-shot
path must meet, and the cheap-vs-faithful note. Restored from
`[deriv-observable-catalog]` (the catalog's "Accuracy regime" column,
dropped in the original CSV transcription). This is the **error-model seed**: each value is a
declared characteristic scale that `arch-10-typeclasses Quantity.combineTol` composes into a
per-residual error budget (`arch-11-residuals В§11.7`). It is a *reference table*, not part of
the lint-enforced atomic tree; the canonical per-formula `Bundle`/`Tier`/`Diff` live in
`physics/library/formulas/registry-manifest.csv`.

"Design-grade" means **device-design-adequate at the UWBG operating point (>500 В°C, high
field)** вҖ” not ab-initio-converged. Where the closed-form path cannot meet design-grade, the
gap is named (and tracked in the audit's P2 list).

**Monoclinic frame guard (ОІ-GaвӮӮOвӮғ вҖ” never relabel).** **Four** distinct axis systems are in play вҖ” the fourth is the elastic-tensor frame `xвҲҘa yвҲҘb zвҲҘc*` (RUS+LDI), whose first axis is the *real* `a`, 13.83В° from `a*`, so `CвӮҒвӮҒ` there is not along the crystal-physical `eвӮҒ` (`[traps]` В§16). Klimm's tensor components are `О»вӮӮвӮӮ вүЎ [010]` and `О»вӮғвӮғ вүЎ [001]`. But his **plane-normal**
measurements `О»вӮҚвӮҒвӮҖвӮҖвӮҺ` and `О»вӮҚвӮҖвӮҖвӮҒвӮҺ` lie along **a\*** and **c\*** and must **never** be

(вүҲ13.8В° off `[001]`) вҖ” which is why the `E_c` / `Оұ_ii` entries below are tagged `c*`, not
`[001]`. In a ~2.5Г—-anisotropic material a relabel silently attaches a value to the wrong
crystallographic direction.
