# Architectural principles

Carried throughout the build:

1. **One compile pipeline over one substrate.** Structure is the 4+1 stage
   compose-time pipeline (`[arch-07-pipeline]`) operating over the
   content-addressed representation substrate (`[arch-20-representations]`).
   The earlier `core ← shared ← …` import chain is retired: it described a
   module layout that no longer exists.
2. **Minimum primitives.** The 12-method computational vocabulary — plus the
   three registered sub-methods (`[arch-09-vocabularies]`) — is the closed
   primitive set; everything else is composition.
3. **No symbolic computation on the runtime path.** Structured data appears only
   as compose-time input or as inert certificate output.
4. **Typed everything.** Every method, template, and formula has an explicit
   typed signature; no string-encoded formulas; no implicit parameters.
5. **Composition over duplication.** Properties are typed compositions of the
   small method vocabulary; observables that share a shape share a template.
6. **Loud at compose time; absent at runtime.** A degeneracy the oracle cannot
   stand behind is caught at compose time and refused with a numeric witness.
   It is never *raised* from the compiled kernel — what the oracle cannot
   certify is simply not in the kernel, so its key is absent from every map
   (`[product]`). At runtime, failure surfaces as a `Failed` cert leaf carrying
   its witness, never as an exception.
7. **Cert is first-class.** Schema + freeze fixture + tamper tripwire +
   high-precision oracle; roughly the weight of any one level.
8. **Numerics-agnostic at the seam, committed within.** The emitted oracle
   assumes nothing about its caller: pure function, flat arrays at the
   boundary, no loop ownership. This is *not* substrate-agnosticism in general
   — internally `/physics` is committed to the substrate of
   `[arch-20-representations]`. It emits state readouts and residuals; the
   integrator, trainer and PINO live downstream, and the time-evolution verbs
   remain unclaimed (`[arch-18-open-decisions]`).
